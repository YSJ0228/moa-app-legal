English

1. How to delete your account

Option A — In-app deletion (instant)

  • Open Moa Moa → Settings tab → Profile → Delete account
  • Re-authenticate with your password (or social login). Deletion is immediate.

Option B — Email request

  • Send an email from the address you registered with to: moa.official.kr@gmail.com
  • Subject: [Moa Moa] Account deletion request
  • We process requests within 7 days.

2. Data that will be deleted

  • Account info (email, nickname, profile photo)
  • All books, expense entries, memos
  • Uploaded receipt images
  • AI chat message history
  • Notification inbox and push tokens
  • Shared book memberships (ownership transferred or book deleted)

3. Data that will be retained

The following are retained for a limited period as required by Korean law, then permanently deleted:

  • E-commerce payment/refund records: 5 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Article 6)
  • Access logs / IP: 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act, Article 15-2)

Personal identifiers (name, email) in the retained data are anonymized.

4. Processing time

  • In-app deletion — real-time (within seconds)
  • Email request — within 7 days of receipt

5. Contact

Questions about account or data deletion: moa.official.kr@gmail.com
